Royal Reels Privacy Policy – Your Data, Clearly Protected

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

At Royal Reels, we place the highest importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy aims to clearly explain how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our website based in the Australia. We are committed to complying with applicable US privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other state regulations.

What Data We Collect and How It Powers Your Experience

To provide you with the best possible services, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal information provided voluntarily: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and other information necessary to provide our services.
  • Browsing data: We collect information about your interaction with our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and visit duration.
  • Location data: With your consent, we may collect information about your location to personalize the services offered.
  • Payment information: Financial transactions are processed by secure payment providers, and we do not store your credit card data.

This data allows us to:

  1. Personalize your user experience according to your preferences

  2. Continuously improve our services and features

  3. Provide you with effective and personalized customer support

  4. Ensure the security of your account and prevent fraud

  5. Inform you of relevant news and offers (with your consent)

The Essential Role of Cookies in Personalizing Royal Reels

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that enhance your experience on our site. We use different types of cookies:

Cookie Type Purpose Retention Period
Essential Cookies Basic site functionality (login, cart) Session or 12 months
Performance Cookies Site usage analysis and improvement 24 months
Functional Cookies Remembering your preferences 12 months
Targeting Cookies Personalized advertising (with consent) 12 months

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit some features of our site.

How We Responsibly Use Your Data to Improve Our Services

We use your data in strict compliance with the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contract performance: To provide the services you requested and manage your account.
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure the security of our platform.
  • Consent: To send you marketing communications when you have explicitly agreed to receive them.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable US laws and regulations.

We only process your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with legal requirements.

Strict Protocols for Data Sharing with Trusted Partners

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with:

  1. Service providers: Partners who help us operate our site (hosting, analytics, payment) under strict confidentiality.

  2. Legal authorities: When required by US law or in the context of legal proceedings.

  3. Business partners: Only with your prior explicit consent.

All our partners are carefully selected and contractually bound to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Your Rights to Access, Modify, or Delete Your Data at Any Time

Under the CCPA and other US privacy laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Know: Obtain confirmation that we process your data and access it.
  • Right to Delete: Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of your personal information.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Exercise your rights without fear of discrimination.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.

To exercise these rights, contact our Privacy Team at privacy@ajiramen.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

Keeping You Informed – Timely Updates to Privacy Policies

We are committed to informing you of any material changes to this privacy policy. Updates will be posted on this page with the date of the last revision. For significant changes, we will notify you by email or via a visible notice on our site.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
